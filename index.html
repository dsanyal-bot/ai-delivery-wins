Win #1 — AI Flow Optimizer Workshop

Rajan Julka T&P APAC · EMpact Program May 2026
The Problem Engineering Managers in T&P had no data-driven view to identify delivery bottlenecks — relying on gut feel, not real flow data from GUS and Git.
What We Did Facilitated the AI Flow Optimizer workshop at EMpact (Hyderabad, May 13–14) for ~40 EMs — using a Claude Code skill that pulls live GUS + Git data to generate a custom flow dashboard per team, then guides EMs through bottleneck identification, root cause analysis, and designing a concrete improvement experiment.
The Outcome EMs who previously spent hours manually pulling delivery data now get their team's full flow dashboard in minutes — walking away with data-backed experiment designs directly tied to 3X productivity goals.
~40 EMs enabled Hours → Minutes Data-backed experiments

Manjari Kumar HCC · AI Champions Circle Q1 FY27
The Problem HCC had no structured mechanism for grassroots AI adoption — tool enablement, risk awareness, and team agreements were inconsistent across AMER and APAC with no accountability at team level.
What We Did Launched and operationalized the HCC AI Champions Circle from scratch (March 11, 2026) — designed the nomination framework, onboarded 18 Champions across AMER + APAC, embedded them as owners of AI tool adoption (Claude, Cursor, PRizm), AI Risk Registers, and Team Agreement refreshes. Institutionalized a 15-min AI Demo showcase in monthly Open Mic.
The Outcome AI tool adoption jumped 9.5 points in one quarter, with a simultaneous surge in developer output — proving that structured, accountable adoption programs deliver compounding results.

Wins #4 & #5 — Service Cloud · ITSM

Kalyan Vandana Service Cloud · ITSM · Scrum Agent May 27 · Tech COO Town Hall
The Problem ITSM scrum teams spent 15–20 min per standup manually pulling GUS status, blockers, and context across multiple tools — causing context-switching overhead and inconsistent sprint visibility.
What We Did Partnered with the ITSM engineering team to build and deploy the Scrum Agent — a Claude Code-powered agent that automates daily standups by pulling live GUS data, surfacing blockers, and delivering structured sprint intelligence directly in Slack.
The Outcome Standup prep reduced from 15–20 minutes to minutes. The Scrum Agent was featured at the Tech COO Town Hall (May 27) as a flagship 3X productivity example — org-wide leadership visibility for APAC AI Delivery.
15–20 min → Minutes Featured · Tech COO Town Hall
